## Who We Are

**KAPS LOCK™** is a small collective that meets in Discord calls and empty classrooms to build things over a weekend that probably shouldn't exist by Monday.

Security tools. Strange interfaces. AI experiments. Weird automation. Systems that talk to other systems they probably shouldn't.

No roadmap. No funding. Just a shared appetite for shipping something real before the coffee runs out.

---

## What We Build

- Offensive & defensive security tools
- AI agents and autonomous systems
- Developer tools
- Experimental interfaces
- Automation platforms
- Open-source infrastructure
- Anything that's fun enough to finish in a weekend

---

## How We Work

- Weekend hackathons
- Build first, polish later
- Small teams, rapid iteration
- Ship early
- Learn by making
- Open source whenever possible

---

## Principles

- **Build over talk**
- **Ship over perfection**
- **Curiosity over convention**
- **Real projects over tutorials**
- **Experiment without permission**
