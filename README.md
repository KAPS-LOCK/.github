## About Us

KAPS LOCK is a student developer collective built around one idea: the best way to learn is to ship under pressure. We form hackathon squads, prototype ambitious hardware-software hybrids and AI systems, and turn 24–48 hour sprints into working products — not just slide decks.

We don't do "eventually." We do "by the deadline."

## What We Do

- 🏆 **Hackathons** — We compete as rotating squads across sponsor tracks, from AI agents to dev tooling to infra automation.
- 🛠 **Rapid Prototyping** — AI pipelines, orchestration systems, full-stack apps, and the occasional Y2K-themed side project.
- 🤝 **Collective Knowledge** — Recurring contributors, shared lessons, and a growing internal playbook on what actually wins judges over (hint: it's not the UI polish alone).
